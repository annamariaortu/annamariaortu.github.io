ABOUT

I am a PhD student in Geometry at SISSA, Trieste, Italy. I started the fourth and final year in October 2022. My supervisors are Ruadhaí Dervan and Jacopo Stoppa.

RESEARCH

My research focus is on complex geometry, mainly Kähler manifolds with special curvature properties and the link with algebro-geometric stability conditions and moduli problems. Currently, I am studying special Kähler metrics on holomorphic fibrations, their geometry and their moduli spaces.

Article

  • Optimal symplectic connections and deformations of holomorphic submersions. arXiv: 2201.12562. (Advances in Mathematics, Vol 414, 2023. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.aim.2023.108868.)

ACTIVITIES

I completed a Bachelor in Mathematics at the University of Milan "Statale" in 2017. Since then, I live in Trieste, where I did a Master in Mathematics under the supervision of Jacopo Stoppa. In 2022 I spent some time at the University of Cambridge as a visiting PhD student, hosted by Ruadhaí Dervan.

  • In the Academic Year 2022/2023 I co-organize the Junior Geometry and Mathematical Physics seminar (website) at SISSA, together with Lorenzo Sillari and Nicholas Rungi.
  • In 2021 and 2022 I co-organized along with many others the Graduate Inter-university Math seminar (website), online.
